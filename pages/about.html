About Me

Cybersecurity researcher from Australia with a focus on offensive security and red teaming. I'm drawn to the human side of security — social engineering, physical intrusion, understanding where people's trust breaks down — but I'm equally comfortable in a terminal working through boxes on HackTheBox.

Security doesn't stop at the network edge. Lockpicking, wardriving, signal cloning — if it can be bypassed, I want to understand how and why.

Background

Came up through a few desk jobs before committing to cybersecurity full-time. Finished my B.I.T in Information Technology (Cybersecurity & Networking) at Southern Cross University and earned my CompTIA Security+ in 2024. Currently finishing a Master of Cybersecurity (Cloud Security) at the University of Sydney and working toward the AWS Solutions Architect Associate.

The goal is to contribute to Australia's cybersecurity space in a meaningful way — always looking for opportunities where I can keep learning and actually make an impact.

What I'm Into

  • Social Engineering — The human side of security, from phishing to physical intrusion
  • Red Teaming — Full-scope adversary simulation
  • Physical Security — Lockpicking, wardriving, DeAuth, cloning, replay attacks
  • Ethics in Cybersecurity — Responsible disclosure, dual-use tooling, where research meets harm
